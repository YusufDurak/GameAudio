## Game Theme & Mechanics
The player spawns with a weapon. The goal is to eliminate enemies and find the correct path to the Jetpack. After collecting the Jetpack, the player flies up to enter the building from the roof, kills the final enemy, and wins the game.

## Game Feel
The game focuses on the feeling of shooting and the vertical movement mechanics provided by the Jetpack.

## Asset List & Implementation

### 1. Ambience
* **Source:** Original Recording (My Laptop Fan).
* **Processing:** I recorded my laptop fan and processed it in Reaper using filters and EQ to create the background atmosphere.

### 2. SFX (Sound Effects)
* **Weapon Sound:** Used for the gun when the player shoots enemies.
* **Enemy Death:** Plays when enemies are eliminated.
* **Jetpack Sound:** Plays when the player uses the jetpack to fly to the roof.

### 3. UI & Music
* **UI:** Simple sounds for the game interface.
* **Music:** Sci-Fi themed music accompanying the gameplay.
